Welcome To DRK College of Engineering and Technology

The era of success of DRK group of educational institutions has started way back in 2004 when it was established by Mr.D.B Chandra Sekhar Rao in the fond memory of his late father Sri Dasari Rama Kotaiah garu. Mr.D.B Chandra Shekar Rao is an eminent & successful entrepreneur, who has subsequently decided to focus on high quality education with the philanthropic motive, to act as a leader to promote economic & industrial growth of the country. DRKCET belongs to the DRK Educational Society. DRK Group has envisaged significant investment of capital and resources to give shape to the development of the institute, in terms of Infrastructure, academic resources and career development for its students. A vibrant effort has been made to consciously invest to improve the conceptual and technical skill of the students.

Sri. D.B. Chandra Sekhar Rao, The Founder
